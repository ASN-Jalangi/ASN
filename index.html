About Us

Adarsha Shishu Niketan was established on 2nd May 1993 with the motto “Education is both Enjoyable & Effective”. We follow the West Bengal State Board Syllabus and are authorized by the West Bengal Government. The school offers classes from Pre-Nursery to Class Eight and emphasizes holistic education.

Government Authorization:

  • Primary: 511 - SE (EE) / 10M - 93 / 2013
  • Upper Primary: Govt. Memo No: 22 - SC / AIS / NOC / AIS / KTE / 2 / 2017 (Part-1)
  • NOC Memo No: S / R / P - R / 2021 / 06
